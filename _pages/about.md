I'm a PhD student in the <a href="https://cims.nyu.edu/gcl/people.html" target="_blank">Geometric Computing Lab</a> in the Courant Institute at NYU, working on geometry processing for medical image simulations. I also offer tutoring services for ACT exam preparation, online and in-person in the New York City area (<a href="/tutoring/">see here</a>). I was previously a staff researcher and undergraduate assistant in the <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/mcalpineresearchgroup/home" target="_blank">3D Printing Functional Materials and Devices Lab</a> at the University of Minnesota, where I worked on path planning algorithms for 3D bioprinting.
